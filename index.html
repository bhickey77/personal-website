Why I became a developer:

During my career in the mergers and acquisitions field I saw inefficiencies in financial processes, throughout deal executions, and within my own work at KPMG. When I found time, I dipped my toes into the software development waters by exploring Ruby on Rails and full stack web development as a whole.

Finally, I decided to drive the change and innovation I had imagined. I said goodbye to the consulting world in March 2018 and I am currently beginning my path to eliminate inefficiencies and spark innovation in the world through technology.
